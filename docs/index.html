About

Hi! I'm Matthew Mark Ibbetson. I'm a software developer with preference for functional programming and a passion for programming language design and history. My primary languages of interest are Lisp, Elixir, and Rust. Occupationally, I work mostly with TypeScript and C#.

My open-source software projects are largely centered around plaintext workflow tools, such as the dn note-taking tool and the litr literate programming tool . Otherwise, my programming interests aim toward creating soft-real-time, self-healing, scalable, concurrent systems with the BEAM.

Outside of programming, I am an avid music enthusiast and novice audiophile; I enjoy reading and writing short-form literature and occasionally taking pictures with my Fujifilm Instax Mini 40. I have also recently taken to recreational logic and mathematics.

I can be reached via email and be found with the username "mmibbetson" wherever I have an account.
